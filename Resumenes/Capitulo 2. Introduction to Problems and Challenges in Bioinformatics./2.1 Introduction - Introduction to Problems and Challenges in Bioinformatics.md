# 2.1 Introduction 

1. Chapter 1 provided an overview of the basics of molecular biology of relevance to bioinformaticians 
	1. And also introduced some of the initial problems faced by researchers in the area. 

2. This chapter examines current and future challenges in bioinformatics. 
	1. The problem areas and challenges are presented according to the field of molecular biology in which they occur: 
		1. The genome
		2. The transcriptome 
		3. The proteome. 

Also, the recently expanding area of gene silencing and interference technology will be covered
